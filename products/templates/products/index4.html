A B O U T U S

Who are we?

Promedical is an online platform equipped with ranges of features for health care giving using digital technologies to enable access of heatlth information and services in a more convenient way.

We aim to monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of health programs. And allowing availability, accessibility, and quality of health information and services in Tanzania.And it is important to monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of health policies and programs.

Our Mission

To bring better and low cost health care services remotely using online consultation and medical test at home.To enable access of health information through online discussions ,forums while also mapping health centers and their services in google map.

Our Why?

Our community has insufficient health facilities, medical supplies and health workers are in huge demand. To reach the global target an additional 18 million health workers are needed, primarily in low and middle countries.

Our Goals

We aim to ensure universal access to sexual and reproductive health-care services, including for family planning, information and education, and the integration of reproductive health into national strategies and programmes.
