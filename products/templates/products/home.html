About us

Who we are

Promedical is an online platform equipped with range of features for health care giving using digital technologies to enable access of health information and services in a more and convenient way.


Our Mission

To bring better and low cost health care services remotely using online consultation and medical test at home. To enable access of health information through online discussion forums while also mapping health centers and their services in google Map.


Our Goals


  • To promote healthy lives and promote well-being for all using digital technologies
  • To reduce preventable deaths of newborns and children under 5 years of age, with all countries aiming to reduce neonatal mortality to at least as low as 12 per 1,000 live births and under-5 mortality to at least as low as 25 per 1,000 live births through reproductive health care provision.
  • To ensure universal access to sexual and reproductive health-care services, including for family planning, information and education, and the integration of reproductive health into national strategies and programmes
  • To reduce spread of the epidemics of AIDs , sexual transmitted infections and other communicable diseases
  • To reduce by one third premature mortality from non-communicable diseases through prevention and treatment and promote mental health and well-being.

Our Why?

Our community has insufficient health facilities, medical supplies and health workers are in huge demand. To reach the global target an additional 18 million health workers are needed, primarily in low and middle countries. To bridge that gape we are inspired to start promoting health care to the community around us by making well-informed choices and enable access of health services in a simple and more digitalized way to reduce the gap.
